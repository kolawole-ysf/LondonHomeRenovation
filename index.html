OUR SERVICES

Services We’re Offering

Our specialized knowledge helps us provide clients with excellent services they can
trust. We offer a great deal of flexibility for customized work.

Home Addition

When it comes to home addition services or repair we offer different services for your different needs. It can be an attic, basement, garage, or sunroom. You can choose to do a home addition project to gain more living space, or if you would like to increase the value of your home. London Home Renovation will help make sure that the job is done right and on time.

Kitchen Renovation

If you are considering renovating your home, the kitchen is one of the best places to invest money into remodeling. A beautiful kitchen can drastically increase the appeal of a home, as well as make a significant impact on your day-to-day life. Having a well laid out, modern kitchen can make cooking a much more enjoyable activity.

Exterior Renovation

At London Home Renovation, we have recommendations for your house’s exterior. We can transform your home from grim and gloomy to lively and glamorous. Moreover, we restore your house’s structure, paying attention to signs of damage and deterioration. To do this, we only use the highest quality materials certified to last you a lifetime.

Basement Renovation

If you have an unfinished basement that is simply serving as a makeshift storage for odds and ends, you are allowing a lot of valuable real estate go to waste. We can help you renovate your basement and convert it into a usable living space that can accommodate additional bedrooms, bathrooms, or recreational areas. There are endless ways to turn a dark and dingy basement into a welcoming space for your family to enjoy.

Flooring

The perfect choice of flooring can serve as the aesthetic foundation for all your home design and decor decisions. With so many different types of flooring materials available, it is easy to imbue your home with a unique personality simply by replacing the existing flooring in each room. We can assist homeowners with the installation or repair of any type of flooring, like hardwood, tiles, carpet laminate, and vinyl.

Bathroom Renovation

Bathroom renovations are also a popular choice for homeowners who are looking to invest in home improvements that will offer them the most satisfaction and enjoyment. A remodeled bathroom can provide you with a luxurious place to unwind after a long and difficult day as well offer better functionality and practicality to make your daily routines easier.
